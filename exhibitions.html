Explore Our 6 Magical Exhibitions

Discover, Learn, and Be Inspired

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Robotics and AI

The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence exhibition showcases the latest advancements in these exciting fields. Interact with advanced robots and AI systems, and learn about their applications in daily life and industry.

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on Earth and how species have evolved over time. Explore the journey from the earliest forms of life to the diversity we see today.

Ecology

Unpack the challenges our planet faces, including global warming and pollution. Learn about sustainable solutions and how we can contribute to a brighter future.

The Art Studio

Unleash your creativity in our Art Studio. Engage in hands-on activities like painting, drawing, and sculpting. Experiment with different media and techniques.

Dinosaurs

Travel back in time to the era of the dinosaurs. Explore life-size models, fossils, and interactive displays to learn about these magnificent creatures.
