Get Involved with Us

For Kids

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do. Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on Saturdays between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts. During the school holidays, we run special holiday clubs with new themes each time. To join, send us a message.

For Teachers

As a community-driven museum, we collaborate with schools to create engaging learning environments. Our team provides guided tours, hands-on activities in our learning laboratory, and inspiring video presentations tailored for your students.

Understanding the importance of science education and budget constraints, we offer flexible programs to accommodate your needs. Contact us to arrange a visit and explore the educational opportunities we provide.

Researchers

Are you looking to collaborate with our team of researchers and academics? Whether you're a student seeking research experience or a professional researcher needing laboratory access, we provide the resources you need to advance your work.

With established partnerships with universities nationwide, we offer unique opportunities to engage in cutting-edge research projects. Contact us to learn more about how you can contribute to our scientific community.
