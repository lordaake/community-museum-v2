Visit Our Museum

Entrance is free for all!

We Offer Guided Tours

  • Guided tours depart every hour.
  • 70 NOK per person, includes a printed museum guide.
  • Group tours for 6+ available upon request.
  • Payment accepted at the museum.

Food and Drink

Enjoy a variety of light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks, and more at our attached café. Our café provides a comfortable and welcoming space to relax and recharge after exploring our exhibits.

We prioritize sustainability by sourcing our ingredients locally, ensuring that you can feel good about the food you're enjoying.

Shop

Our shop offers a wide range of memorabilia, science-themed gifts, and educational materials that allow you to continue exploring science even after your visit. Whether you're looking for a souvenir or an engaging activity pack, our shop has something for everyone.

Visit us to pick up a unique gift or browse our selection of science-inspired merchandise.

Accessibility

Our museum is fully equipped with wheelchair accessibility ramps, ensuring that all visitors can enjoy our exhibits comfortably. We are committed to providing an inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of ability.

If you have any specific accessibility needs, please contact us prior to your visit. We will make every effort to accommodate your requirements and ensure a pleasant experience.
